## Pre-Tuned Defaults
- Temperature: 0.1 · Top-k: 5 · Hybrid weights: 60/40 (vector/keyword)
- Chunking: 512 tokens, semantic strategy, 10% overlap
- Guardrails: Groundedness ≥0.8, Relevance ≥0.7, Content Safety enabled
